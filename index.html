Resolving Cash Flow Challenges with Initial Billing & Pre-Scrubbing Services

Background

  • A prominent dental practice in Denver, CO, was facing significant cash flow issues due to inefficiencies in their billing and claims management processes.
  • The practice was struggling with delays in claims submissions, frequent claim rejections, and an overall inefficient billing workflow.
  • The practice sought to improve its financial stability by optimizing its billing processes.
  • Location: Denver, CO
  • Practice Type: Family Dentistry
  • Number of Locations: 1
  • Number of Providers: 4
  • Patient Volume: 900 patients per month

Challenges

  • Cash Flow Crunch: The practice faced a severe cash flow crunch due to delays in claim submissions and payments, leading to difficulties in meeting operational expenses and investing in growth opportunities.
  • High Claim Rejection Rates: Claims were often rejected due to errors or missing information, causing additional administrative work and prolonged revenue realization.
  • Inefficient Initial Billing: The process for generating and submitting initial bills was time-consuming and prone to errors, further contributing to delays in cash flow.
  • Administrative Overload: The in-house billing team was overwhelmed with the volume of claims and billing issues, affecting overall productivity and patient service.

Solution

The Dentistry Clinic engaged CEC to implement our Initial Billing and Pre-Scrubbing services. The solution focused on the following key areas:

  • Initial Billing Services: Our team took over the responsibility of generating and submitting initial bills for dental services. This included ensuring that all necessary information was accurately captured and that bills were sent promptly to insurance companies.
  • Pre-Scrubbing Services: We provided comprehensive pre-scrubbing of claims before submission. This process involved reviewing claims for accuracy, completeness, and compliance with payer requirements to minimize the risk of rejections and denials.
  • Workflow Optimization: We assessed and optimized the existing billing workflow to streamline processes and reduce bottlenecks, enhancing overall efficiency.
  • Ongoing Support and Reporting: Our team provided regular updates and reports on the status of claims and billing activities, offering insights and recommendations for continuous improvement.

Results

  • Enhanced Cash Flow: By taking over the initial billing and pre-scrubbing processes, CEC significantly improved Bright Smiles Family Dentistry’s cash flow. The time from service delivery to payment decreased by 35%, resulting in a more stable and predictable revenue stream.
  • Reduced Claim Rejections: The pre-scrubbing services led to a 45% reduction in claim rejections due to errors or missing information. This improvement translated into faster payments and less time spent addressing claim issues.
  • Increased Billing Efficiency: The practice experienced a 50% improvement in billing efficiency. Our team’s handling of initial billing and pre-scrubbing allowed the practice’s administrative staff to focus on other critical tasks and patient care.
  • Operational Improvement: With an optimized billing workflow, the practice reported a reduction in administrative overload, leading to a smoother operation and enhanced productivity.

Conclusion

The partnership between Dentistry Clinic and CEC highlights the effectiveness of specialized Initial Billing and Pre-Scrubbing services in resolving cash flow challenges. By addressing key issues in the billing process and implementing targeted solutions, CEC helped the practice achieve improved financial stability, reduced claim rejections, and enhanced operational efficiency.
